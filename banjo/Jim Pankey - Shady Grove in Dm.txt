Shady Grove in Dm
Jim Pankey https://youtu.be/yF_7jUPfGN4

Standard banjo tuning but with the fifth string capoed to A. You can tune up if you don't have railroad spikes or a 5th string capo, but that's about as far as you'd want to try tuning up in lieu of a capo.
When you come down from the 10th fret there are other picking combinations that will work at 6 and 7 since the 5th string is also capoed at 7, but it's simplest to keep it the same as what you do next at the 5th fret and otherwise the same string has to be struck sequentially.

This is how he teaches it, but he seems to play it fast like this:
d|-----0-                  d|--0----
B|--2s3--                  B|--2-s3-
    i  m                       im

Tuning: aDGBd
Key of Dm
