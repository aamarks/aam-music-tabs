Sam Beam & Jesca Hoop: NPR Music Tiny Desk Concert

The three songs in this session were all done in DADGAD tuning, but tuned down 3 more steps to B, for some really loose, percussive strings. Thanks to Einar Aassved for coming up with the tuning in a youtube comment.

3. Every Songbird Says

When playing the 550400 sometimes he leaves it plucking the second string and sometimes he pluck/pulls the 5th string (an octave lower) as he releases the chord to move to 000200. Note that the 2nd and 3rd string are playing the same note on the 000200 so it doesn't really matter which one you pluck.
